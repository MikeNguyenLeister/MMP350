Aloha

Lava Boards © is a startup selling contemporary, skateboard merchandise from Hawaiian, Filipino, and Japanese brands while promoting a culture of inclusiveness, equality, sustainability, optimism, freedom, love, and authenticity.

As they say in Hawaii, "Nānā i ke kumu" or "know your truth."


Grid Designs

Grid Design 1 is made to be a home page layout. Banner would include the logo/title and search bar; slider would advertise new products; sidebar would let users customize search criteria by category and build their skateboard; footer would allow for other promotional materials, and main and content would explain content, show off products and store news, and link user to the store gallery.
