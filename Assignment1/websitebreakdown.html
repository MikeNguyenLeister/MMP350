Website Breakdown - MMP 350

Welcome to my webpage! I'm Mike Nguyen Leister and taking Advanced Web Design or MMP350 this spring at the Borough of Manhattan Community College. In summary, I'll break down how to create an account and log in on Disney + . Enjoy!


I. Visit www.disneyplus.com

This will take you the main page. Once you arrive, click the LOG IN button on the upper right.


II. Sign up

Below the continue button, find the New to Disney+? Sign up. Click the sign up button.


III. Email

Enter your email and click the AGREE & CONTINUE button.

IV. Password

Create your password based on the required parameters of 6 characters (case sensitve) with at least one number or special character.

V. Payment

Choose your payment plan, add your credit card data, and click AGREE & SUBSCRIBE button.

VI. Log In

Log in and click the CONTINUE button.

VII. Profile

Modify your profile by clicking on the profile button in the upper right of the screen.


Click any anything in the dropdown menu to update your profile.


VIII. Account

Update your account at any time by clicking the account button in the profile drop-down on the upper right of the screen.


IX.Add Movies

Add movies or shows to your profile by first clicking the search button on the upper left and then typing or clicking on the movie or show of your choice.



Click the + button.


X. Profile Avatar

Go back to the profile drop-down menu and click the profile + button to find a profile avatar. Click on any image that you want as your avatar.


Click the SAVE button.


XI.App Settings

Go back to the profile drop-down menu and click the App Settings button to modify your app setting that best works for your device. Click the SAVE.


Conclusion

There are many other neat features to the Disney + steaming service, but once you sign up and play around with the app, you'll find all the cool, additional features and content at your disposal!
